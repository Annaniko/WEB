Variety of dogs

About the breeds

Diversity

There are currently about 400 dog breeds. Many breeds disappear over time, but new breeds are constantly emerging instead. The variety of dog uses and the tastes of dog breeders contributed to the emergence of different breeds. Diversity in dog breeding allowed that individual breeds differ from each other not only in color, coat shape and body structure, but also in size and weight.

Purpose of breeds

Initially, the vast majority of breeds originated as hunting dogs. Different stages of development of hunting and different conditions have necessitated the appropriate type of dog. But, besides hunting, the dog from the very beginning of its coexistence with man had another purpose - it guards his home. For many generations, some breeds of dogs only encountered their domestic people, while they were distrustful and vicious towards strangers. By purposeful breeding of distrustful and vicious dogs to strangers, modern breeds of service dogs were bred.
